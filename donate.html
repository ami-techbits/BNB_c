💖 Donate to the Needy Students Fund

Not every student can afford the basics — but every student deserves the chance to learn. The BNB Needy Students Fund provides immediate and ongoing support to learners facing hardship, so that they never have to choose between hunger and homework.

Your donation — no matter the size — helps us provide uniforms, meals, school supplies, transportation, and tuition coverage. 100% of contributions go directly to students in need, guided by our transparent and trusted selection process.

You’re not just donating to a fund — you’re removing barriers, restoring dignity, and opening doors to a brighter future.
