🌟 The GAP Bright Girls Scholarship

The GAP Bright Girls Scholarship is more than financial support — it's a commitment to equity, empowerment, and excellence. This program identifies high-potential young women and provides them with access to quality education, mentorship, and leadership opportunities within BNB Learning Center.

Through the GAP initiative, we invest in the minds and hearts of girls who will shape communities, industries, and futures. Together, we’re building a pathway for bright girls to become brilliant leaders.

💡 Our Mission & Impact

At BNB, we believe that when you empower a girl through education, you change generations. The GAP Bright Girls Scholarship isn’t just about removing financial barriers — it’s about unlocking leadership, amplifying voice, and lighting the spark of possibility in every scholar we reach.

Since its founding, the initiative has supported dozens of young women — many of whom are now excelling in university, leading in their communities, and mentoring the next generation of girls following in their footsteps.

✅ Eligibility & Application Requirements

The GAP Bright Girls Scholarship is open to young women who show strong academic potential, leadership qualities, and a passion for learning.

📚 Academic Performance

Applicants must demonstrate consistent academic achievement in core subjects. Report cards or transcripts from the past year are required.

🌟 Leadership & Character

Active involvement in school, community, or personal development programs is a strong advantage.

📄 Required Documents

- Completed application form
- Recent school report card
- Personal statement (max 500 words)
- Parent/guardian ID & contact details

🗓️ Application Deadline

Applications must be submitted by August 15th for the following academic year. Late submissions may not be considered.

📝 How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to complete your application and take your first step toward an empowered future.

📥 Step 1: Download the Application

Get the official application form from our website or admissions office. Carefully review all required fields.

🖊️ Step 2: Fill Out the Form

Complete all sections of the form. Include your academic background, personal statement, and guardian details.

📎 Step 3: Gather Your Documents

Prepare your most recent school report card, ID copies, and your written statement. Combine them with your form.

📨 Step 4: Submit Your Application

Send the completed application to the BNB Admissions Office or upload it through our online portal before the deadline.
