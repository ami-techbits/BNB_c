🤲 Parents’ Support to Needy Students

At BNB, we are more than a school — we are a community. And in our community, no child should ever be left behind. Through the Parents’ Support to Needy Students initiative, families who are able to give extend a helping hand to those facing financial difficulty, ensuring that education remains a shared opportunity.

This powerful peer-to-peer support system allows parents to contribute toward tuition, materials, or meals — turning kindness into academic success, and generosity into transformation.

💡 United by Purpose

The Parents’ Support initiative is grounded in the belief that when one child succeeds, we all succeed. By pooling resources and compassion, BNB families are building more than classrooms — they're building community, equity, and shared achievement.

Every birr contributed is a seed of possibility — helping a child attend school, feel included, and realize their potential. This is what it means to lift as we rise.

📦 Where Your Support Goes

Every contribution, no matter how big or small, directly impacts a student’s ability to learn with dignity and hope.

🎓 Full or Partial Tuition

Helps cover school fees for students who otherwise would not be able to attend or continue their education at BNB.

🧥 Uniforms & Supplies

Provides essentials like shoes, backpacks, stationery, and clean school uniforms to ensure students feel prepared and proud.

🍲 Daily Meals

Supports access to nutritious school meals so no child has to learn while hungry — because focus starts with food.

🚍 Transportation

Enables supported students to travel to and from school safely and consistently, especially from remote areas.

🤲 How You Can Make a Difference

Whether it’s covering a child’s lunch, contributing to a uniform, or sponsoring tuition, your support echoes far beyond the classroom. Join a growing network of BNB families helping one another — because every child deserves a chance to thrive.
