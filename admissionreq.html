Admission Requirements

Everything you need to begin your BNB journey — clearly outlined, carefully considered.

Our admission process is designed to be accessible, transparent, and supportive of every family. Whether you're enrolling for the first time or transferring from another school, we provide clear guidelines to help you gather the right documents and prepare your child for a smooth transition.

Requirements vary slightly by grade level, but every application is reviewed with care and attention. Our team is here to guide you through each step — from paperwork to placement.

Download Checklist

Preparing for Admission

At BNB Learning Center, preparation is key to a successful admission journey. We encourage students and families to start early, gather essential documents, and familiarize themselves with the expectations for each grade level.

From practicing literacy and numeracy basics to preparing for entrance interviews, applicants should approach the process with confidence and curiosity. Parents are encouraged to communicate openly with the admissions team and to support their child’s emotional readiness and academic interest.

For older students, especially those entering middle and high school, it’s helpful to reflect on personal goals, study habits, and subject interests, as this will form a part of the conversation during the admissions process.

BNB Learning Center Admission Requirements

Placement from Kindergarten to Grade 12 based on readiness, values, and academic potential.

Kindergarten (KG1 – KG3)

  • Age-appropriate placement (starting from age 4)
  • Developmental readiness screening
  • Parent meeting for social-emotional evaluation

Primary School (Grades 1 – 6)

  • Completion of previous grade
  • BNB entrance assessment (literacy & numeracy)
  • Interview with student and parent(s)

Middle School (Grades 7 – 8)

  • Academic transcript in core subjects
  • Written entrance test (English, Math, Aptitude)
  • Family interview for academic/cultural fit

High School (Grades 9 – 12)

  • Strong academic record
  • Entrance exams (English, Math, Science)
  • Personal essay & interview
